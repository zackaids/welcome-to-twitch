How to Begin Streaming?

  1. Review the community guidelines

    You should read the Twitch community guidelines before you sign up for Twitch.

  2. Create an account

    On a desktop computer, click the Sign up and fill out the form. On mobile devices, download the Twitch mobile app.

  3. Choose which software you will use

    OBS Studio - OBS Studio is a free and open source software for video recording and live streaming. It is also the most popular streaming software.

    Streamlabs - Streamlabs is a more user-friendly alternative to OBS. It is also free and open source.
